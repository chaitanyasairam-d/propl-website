What is this book about?

It is inevitable that every business will become a digital business. However, it is discouraging to see that 70% of transformations failed to deliver value.

  • What if every organization has the right constructs to extract true value of digital?
  • What if every organization’s digital efforts become a roaring success?
It is my humble attempt, through this book, to make that future happen. During my experience advising and driving digital transformations globally, and from countless interactions with Senior Executives, I have seen and experienced business challenges firsthand during digital transformations. As we solved these challenges, I saw six broad themes emerge aka ‘The Six Digital-first Realizations’. To explain these Digital-first Realizations and many other insights, I chose to write a fable. Perhaps the first business fable in digital transformation.

Who should read this book?

  • CEOs, Managing Directors

    As a leader, you’re expected to define the competitive advantage and rally the entire company to achieve it. This book is for you if you have struggled to define the strategy or align the teams to extract true value of digital.

  • Executives, Product Leaders

    It takes a village to raise a child, so does driving digital transformation successfully. This book is for you if you want to understand the nuances of driving digital transformations and build winning teams.

  • Individual Contributors

    Every individual will contribute to digital initiatives, one way or the other. This book is for you if want your efforts to deliver game changing outcomes. Learn how to innovate with digital and be future-ready.
